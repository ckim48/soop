Bridging the Emotional Support Gap for LGBTQ+ Youth

Soop offers a secure, anonymous environment where individuals can confidently manage their emotional health, overcome stigma, and navigate identity issues. With an empathy-driven design, we ensure that those who need support the most receive it in a personalized, accessible way.

  • AI-powered emotional support tailored to individual needs
  • Focused on LGBTQ+ youth in North America and Asia
  • Anonymous and secure environment to express and heal

Our Mission

We are dedicated to expanding access to mental healthcare by providing personalized, empathetic support to marginalized individuals. Our platform empowers users to confidently address emotional struggles and thrive in today’s digital environment.
